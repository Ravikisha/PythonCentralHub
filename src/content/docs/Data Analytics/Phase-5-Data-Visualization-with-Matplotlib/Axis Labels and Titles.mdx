## Always label your charts

Without labels, your chart is incomplete.

```python title="Labels" showLineNumbers{1}
import matplotlib.pyplot as plt

x = [1, 2, 3, 4]
y = [100, 120, 90, 150]

plt.figure(figsize=(7, 4))
plt.plot(x, y, marker="o")
plt.title("Daily orders")
plt.xlabel("Day")
plt.ylabel("Orders")
plt.tight_layout()
plt.show()
```

## Include units

Examples:

- Revenue (₹)
- Duration (seconds)
- Conversion rate (%)

## Tip

A good title explains the question, not just the chart type.

Bad: "Line chart"

Better: "Daily orders increased after campaign launch"
